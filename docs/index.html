Age of Agatha

A medieval fantasy MMORPG
By: Phillip Lorentz and Erich Heisler

Features

Below are a few of our major milestones that we are hoping to have as elements in Age of Agatha.

Setting

Medieval Fantasy; this means multiple races like humans, elves and orcs. This also implies things like magick, early firearms, and possibly steampunk airships.

Lineage

In our game, death is death. We are not so mean to take everything away though. You will be given some boosts to help you quickly recover but this should be enough to scare you from dieing.

Combat

We appreciate the intense combat from games like mount and blade but also more relaxing games like runescape. Ours will try to bridge this gap.

Classes

Content should never be locked out from a player based on classes but classes will still have meaning. Certain classes will just naturally be better at certain things.

Warfare

There will be war between the player kingdoms and an NPC only kingdom. This is to stop players from being locked out of content due to losing a war but still have the fun of faction warfare.

Quests

There will be some dynamically created quests like "X lord goes to war with Y lord, player talks to Z person serving in X's fief to help X with the war effort."

Activities

Overall our primary focus is going to be on activities in Age of Agatha. This does not mean we will not devote time to world building or character development but the majority of our focus will be toward giving the players more in the way of mechanics and actions they can do within their world.

Team

We are currently a confirmed team size of 2 members. Hopefully we can increase this size but for now this should be fine.
