Wings3D is bundled with AgeOfAgatha as the recomended modeling software for development.
The 'src' folder contains the source files at the time this portable version was made.
This version was made for windows 10 and could have unexpected behavior on other devices.
Models in AgeOfAgatha should be stored in wavefront .obj and .mtl files

Link to Wings3D website: http://www.wings3d.com/
Link to Wings3D github: https://github.com/dgud/wings

At the time of inclusion to AgeOfAgatha: "Wings 3D is open source and completely free for use in both personal and commercial projects." -source: http://www.wings3d.com/
